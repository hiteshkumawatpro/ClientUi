THE REMARKABLY YOU PROGRAM

USING AVERAGE SKILLS TO BUILD A TOWERING PERSONAL BRAND!

WHO IS IT FOR?

This workshop is for passionate executives and managers who know that they are damn good at what they
do and want to do meaningful work but struggle to make an impression or be known for something 

HOW DOES IT WORK

The Building Your Personal Brand Workshop has 3 elements.

Element 1

PREPRATION :
*The seed can grow so much better if the soil is first prepared for it. We begin preparing the soil of your mind by - getting to know you better - we provide you with self -audits to understand your personality type, your signature strengths and your glorious weaknesses (we will tell you why we call them glorious when you meet us) - and how they can contribute to your personal brand sharing a few of the premises the workshop is based on so that we can jump into applying the concepts as soon as possible.

Element 2

THE WORKSHOP

2. Understanding the paradigms of adding value to
people

4. Making your work standout
5. Building your credibility within your
organization
6. Refining your brand continually
7. Networking for introverts
8. How ordinary skills can get you extraordinary
results
9. Working smart not just hard
10. Doing Remarkable Work and finding fulfilment
even if you choose the rat race

ESSENCE OF THE PROGRAM

Mukesh Ambani did it, Le Bron James did it and so did Oprah But are you doomed if you're not one of them or like them? Can only these 'kind of people command a powerful personal brand?
In Remarkably You - Creating your personal brand - we set out to examine these questions. After interviewing hundreds of successful people from the slums of Nizamuddin to large corporate CEOs, to very successful entrepreneurs many of whom haven't even had a chance at a full-fledged education, we've uncovered what's worked for them. We bring this to you in a step-by-step process, which could allow you to very strategically create your personal brand We've seen this process work time and time again We can show you how it can work for you.

IN THIS PROGRAM YOU WILL LEARN HOW TO

1. Use your strengths as well as your weakness (yes weakness have a place too and we will tell you how to use them)
to create your personal brand
2. Create and flourish in a niche of your making, where you're at the top - from day 1, instead of competing in already
crowded spaces
Leverage your personal brand to grow higher, faster and in a more fulfilling way whether you are employed or run your
own outfit
